Education

  • I couldn't quite remember how was my nursery but I know it should had been a stress free and a excting one.

  • I couldn't quite remember how was my kindergarten too but then I know it should also had been a fun and enjoyable one.

  • 2006

    I was in primary school in 2006 when I was 6 years old. I still remember during the first day of primary school I was very afraid of what was coming ahead of me and I am not very used to the environment. However, thanks to the school's buddy system where a primary 4 student will pair up with a primary one student and help them to get used to primary school life, I was gradually able to adapt to primary school and make a lot of friends. In the end, I graduated primary with a PSLE T-score of 235 which I think is a decent score.

  • 2012

    Secondary School had been one of the most toughest education journey that I had experienced. My secondary school life had been full of ups and downs. Ups such as me able to have a lot of great friends have a long lasting friendship with them. Downs such as the mountains of homework needed to do yet with short deadlines. The most stressful part of secondary life is especially secondary 3 and 4 where we need to start preparing for O levels. This is because the teachers want to cover all the O levels content as soon as possible. Teaching had thus become fast paced and I found it very difficult to cope. Thankfully, I persevered on and mangaged to finish my secondary school life and graduated with a L1R5 of 11 points.

  • 2016

    So currently, I am a student in Singapore Polytechnic. I think this is the best part of my education journey. Even though, sometimes it can be quite stressful due lots of work to do, polytechnic life is overall still an enjoyable and fun one. Let's just say in poly, you are able to work hard and play hard.
